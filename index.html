The site gathers information about cryptocurrency. On the home page, are displayed all the coins from CoinGecko, our information provider.

For each coin you can get more information by clicking the buttons and can filter your display using the search bar.

Furthermore, you can add up to five coins to your favourites and track those favourites in the report section.

Hope you enjoy the site, and please feel free to contact me and send ideas for improvements and information on any bugs you come across.
